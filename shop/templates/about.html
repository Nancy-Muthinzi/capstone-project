Who We Are

The Jewel Shop is more than just a shop. We are a gateway to empowerment. We believe that charitable efforts, while noble, do not provide a long-term solution to poverty. Instead, economic development and job growth are the key to eliminating poverty for good. Our mission is to create steady, fair-waged jobs for our partner artisans in Kenya. Every product directly impacts the community and family of the artisans who made it.
