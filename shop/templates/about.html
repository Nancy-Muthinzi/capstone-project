CHANGE STARTS WITH EMPOWERMENT

We want to end poverty. But we believe the people best equipped to help communities are those who are a part of them. Rather than enacting temporary relief measures or spending money on short-term aid, we want to invest in communities so that they are empowered to create change for themselves. Our mission is to empower resourceful, hard-working people in Kenya to earn long-term, sustainable incomes and, in doing so, fight poverty. Our model: We use the fashion industry to bring a large market to artisans in Kenya who would otherwise have little access to buyers. We work with our partners in Kibera, Nairobi to create designs relevant to today's fashion market.


A New Opportunity

We create jobs by bringing our partner artisans' craft of paper beaded jewelry to a global market.

A Steady Income

Having a consistent source of income changes everything. Our partners in Uganda are able to meet their immediate needs.

A Bright Future

As they continue to work with Tuli, our partners are able to put aside long-term savings and build a better future for themselves.

The Jewel Store is all about long-term solutions. Rather than enacting temporary relief efforts, your purchase creates long-term jobs and steady incomes to break the cycle of poverty. Our artisans in Kenya are empowered to create their own futures.

We believe in unity among people and collaborative solutions. When you wear a our product, you are joining this community. You are an agent for change.
